# Monetization

## Monetization Strategy

AqariX should not depend only on listing ads. Revenue should come from seller/dealer workflow value, supervised transaction value, investor intelligence, and agency services.

## Revenue Streams

### Seller Free Trial Conversion

Target: individual sellers.

Mechanic:

- Free trial upgrades into paid listing, AI assistant usage, boosts, and agency access.

### Dealer CRM Subscription

Target: brokers and dealer offices.

Mechanic:

- Monthly fee for lead pipeline, listing management, performance analytics, and team workflow.

### Managed Lead Room Fees

Target: sellers, dealers, developers.

Mechanic:

- Fee per qualified lead, viewing, successful transaction, or premium supervised negotiation support.

### Smart Boosts

Target: sellers and dealers.

Mechanic:

- Paid distribution of listings to compatible buyers/investors based on recommendation fit.

Guardrail:

- Sponsored listings must remain compatibility-aware to protect marketplace trust.

### Premium Investor Reports

Target: buyers and investors.

Mechanic:

- Detailed offering analysis, forecast PDF, comparable report, and area opportunity scan.

### B2B Analytics SaaS

Target: developers and institutions.

Mechanic:

- Market heatmaps, demand trends, yield forecasts, API access, and data exports.

### AqariX Agency

Target: sellers, dealers, developers.

Mechanic:

- Paid content production, social media packages, reels, campaign assets, and launch support.

## Suggested Pricing Tiers

| Tier | Monthly Price | Included Value |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Free user | 0 JOD | Basic search, limited valuations, saved listings, entry-level alerts |
| Standard buyer/investor | 3-7 JOD | Unlimited saved searches, comparison tool, richer analysis, forecast watchlist |
| Seller trial | 0 JOD for trial | Limited listings, pricing assistant sample, basic leads, upgrade prompt |
| Seller/Dealer Pro | 15-49 JOD | Listing management, CRM pipeline, boosts, assistant usage, performance dashboard, agency access |
| Developer/Investor SaaS | 99-299 JOD | Market dashboards, forecasts, bulk reports, exports, API access, project lead rooms |
| Agency packages | Per package | Content and campaign services priced by listing, monthly bundle, or project launch |

## Monetization Guardrails

- Do not damage trust with irrelevant sponsored listings.
- Do not hide core buyer trust features behind paywalls too early.
- Do not charge sellers before the trial demonstrates value.
- Do not let agency complexity block marketplace launch.
- Track conversion by role and feature, not only total revenue.
