# User Flows

## Buyer Flow

1. Sign up or log in.
2. Select buyer role.
3. Complete intake: budget, city, property type, family/lifestyle needs, financing status, and preferred timeline.
4. Browse home/map feed.
5. Filter by city, neighborhood, price, area, type, bedrooms, freshness, and verified status.
6. Open offering detail.
7. Review AI analysis: fair value, price gap, bargain range, forecast, location momentum, liquidity, and recommendation.
8. Save, compare, or start a managed lead room.
9. Complete inquiry qualification.
10. Communicate, schedule viewing, make offer, negotiate, and close or archive.

## Investor Flow

1. Sign up or log in.
2. Select investor role.
3. Complete investment intake: budget, risk tolerance, investment horizon, expected rental use, target city/neighborhood, and yield preferences.
4. View ranked offerings.
5. Compare up to four offerings.
6. Review forecast watchlist.
7. If a listing is weak or overpriced, inspect nearby opportunity scout.
8. Start lead room for selected offering.
9. Track offer, negotiation, and outcome.

## Seller Flow

1. Sign up or log in.
2. Select seller role.
3. Activate free trial.
4. Create listing with structured property data and media.
5. Use pricing assistant for suggested asking price, bargain range, comparable evidence, and days-on-market risk.
6. Use marketing assistant for copy, photo guidance, captions, and campaign suggestions.
7. Publish listing or submit for review.
8. Receive qualified inquiries through managed lead rooms.
9. Track lead status, viewings, offers, and outcomes.
10. Upgrade for more listings, assistant usage, boosts, or agency packages.

## Dealer/Broker Flow

1. Sign up or log in.
2. Join or create dealer organization.
3. Activate trial or subscription.
4. Add listings.
5. Monitor listing performance.
6. Manage AqariX-originated leads in CRM pipeline.
7. Use tasks, reminders, message summaries, appointments, and notes.
8. Order Smart Boosts or agency packages.
9. Track conversion and pipeline value.

## Managed Lead Room Flow

Stages:

1. New inquiry: capture buyer intent, property, budget fit, and contact preference.
2. Qualified: confirm seriousness, financing readiness, timeline, and next action.
3. Viewing scheduled: coordinate appointment, reminders, map notes, and attendance.
4. Offer made: record offer amount, reasoning, and comparable/AVM context.
5. Negotiation: track counters, objections, seller constraints, and assistant guidance.
6. Closing support: maintain checklist for documents, payment steps, inspection notes, and broker/admin handoff.
7. Closed: capture final price, lead source, time-to-close, satisfaction, and model calibration data.
8. Archived: preserve loss reason, next best offering, and reactivation reminder.

## Admin Flow

1. Review marketplace KPIs.
2. Monitor quality flags and suspicious behavior.
3. Review risky listings or disputed valuations.
4. Supervise lead rooms when needed.
5. Resolve disputes, spam, abuse, and off-platform leakage flags.
6. Review agency queue health.
7. Export insights for model calibration and operations.

## Agency Operator Flow

1. View assigned agency orders.
2. Check property details, audience, requirements, delivery date, and approval status.
3. Produce listing copy, captions, scripts, campaign assets, or launch materials.
4. Submit assets for approval.
5. Attach approved assets to listing, campaign, or dealer profile.
6. Review performance after launch.
