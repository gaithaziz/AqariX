## Product Positioning

AqariX is a supervised AI real estate marketplace and CRM platform for Jordan. It serves buyers, investors, sellers, dealers/brokers, AqariX admins, and agency operators through role-specific interfaces.

The core promise is:

- Buyers and investors get evidence-backed discovery, valuation, forecasting, and guided negotiation.
- Sellers and dealers get pricing support, better listing marketing, managed lead rooms, and CRM retention.
- AqariX becomes the intelligence layer and transaction coordination layer for Jordanian real estate.

## Build Principle

Do not build AqariX as another property listing board. Build it as a supervised, role-aware, data-backed real estate operating layer with trust, explainability, and workflow retention at the center.
